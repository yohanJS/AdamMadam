Paldrome App

A palindrome is a word, phrase, or other sequence of characters that reads the same backward as forward, such as madam or racecar. We have added a few examples for you to try. We are not checking for numbers in this case.

Single Word Palindromes

  • Anna
  • Civic
  • Kayak
  • Level
  • Redder

Multiple Word Palindromes

  • Don't nod.
  • Red rum, sir, is murder
  • Top spot
  • Was it a cat I saw?
  • No lemon, no melon
